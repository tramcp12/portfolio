About CP12

Not just a bed.
A base camp.

Trạm CP12 was born from a simple frustration: too many Đà Lạt stays felt like transactions. You'd arrive, sleep, leave — and miss the actual city entirely.

We built something different. A place run by locals, designed for connection, and obsessively curated for solo travelers who want the real Đà Lạt — not the brochure version.

🗺
Local expertise, freely shared

Hand-drawn route maps, weekly community boards, and hosts who actually run these trails.

5:30am breakfast, gear drying area, recovery station with electrolytes and local herbal teas.

🤝
Solo-traveler focused

Weekly Trạm Nights, free morning walks Tue & Fri, and a dorm designed so friendships form naturally.

Common Questions

Everything you need to know.

Is parking available?

Yes — free motorbike and car parking is available on-site.

Is breakfast included?

Breakfast is not included. The shared kitchen has a rice cooker, kettle, pots, and fridge — fully usable by all guests.

Is there hot water?

Yes — all rooms have hot water showers with electric heaters. Reliable year-round.

What is the Wi-Fi speed?

Fiber broadband, typically 50–100 Mbps. Solid for remote work and video calls.

What are check-in and check-out times?

Check-in from 14:00. Check-out by 12:00. Early check-in or late check-out is available on request — just message us.

Can I store luggage after check-out?

Yes — free luggage storage until 18:00 on your check-out day. Just let us know.

How do I get here from the bus station?

Grab or xe ôm (motorbike taxi) takes about 15 minutes. Show the driver: 228/6 Đường Phú Đồng Thiên Vương, Phường 8.

What is the deposit and cancellation policy?

We hold your room with a small deposit via bank transfer. Cancellations with 48+ hours notice receive a full refund. Message us on Zalo or Facebook for details.
