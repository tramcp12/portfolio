House Rules

Policies for a
peaceful stay.

🕒

General Rules & Quiet Hours

Quiet Time is strictly enforced from 10:00 PM to 7:00 AM. Please keep voices, music, and TV volumes low. Check-in from 14:00, check-out by 12:00. Turn off AC and lights when leaving.

🍳

Shared Kitchen & BBQ Area

Clean all dishes and the grill immediately after use. Return items to their original places. Do not move furniture. A cleaning penalty of 200,000 VND applies if left uncleaned.

🎤

Karaoke & Entertainment

All Karaoke and loud music must completely stop by 9:30 PM. Please close doors and windows while singing.

🧺

Laundry & Wash Area

Operating hours: 7:00 AM to 9:30 PM. Please promptly remove clothes once the cycle is complete.

🚭

Smoking Policy

Smoking is 100% prohibited indoors. Designated outdoor areas only. Please maintain general cleanliness.

🌲

Garden & Outdoor Areas

Please respect our plants and landscaping. Do not pick flowers or branches. Clean up any trash after enjoying the outdoor spaces.

Common Questions

Everything you need to know.

Is parking available?

Yes — free motorbike and car parking is available on-site.

Is breakfast included?

Breakfast is not included. The shared kitchen has a rice cooker, kettle, pots, and fridge — fully usable by all guests.

Is there hot water?

Yes — all rooms have hot water showers with electric heaters. Reliable year-round.

What are check-in and check-out times?

Check-in from 14:00. Check-out by 12:00. Early check-in or late check-out is available on request — just message us.

Can I store luggage after check-out?

Yes — free luggage storage until 18:00 on your check-out day. Just let us know.

What is the deposit and cancellation policy?

We hold your room with a small deposit via bank transfer. Cancellations with 48+ hours notice receive a full refund. Message us on Zalo or Facebook for details.

Do you rent motorbikes?

We don't rent motorbikes directly, but we can provide trusted local contacts for you to rent from.

Are pets allowed?

Yes, we are pet-friendly! We have a shared pet area. Please bring your own pet accessories and bedding.

Freedom to wander: 24/7 Access

There is no curfew. After check-in, you will be given a password for 24/7 independent access to the homestay.

Mountain chills: Room heating

Yes, our rooms are equipped with heaters to keep you warm during the chilly Đà Lạt nights.
